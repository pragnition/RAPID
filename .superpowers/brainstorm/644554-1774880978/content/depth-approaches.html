Adding Depth to the Diagrams

Three approaches to making lifecycle-flow and agent-dispatch feel polished instead of flat

A

Subtle Shadows + Gradients

Add soft drop shadows behind nodes/pills and use top-to-bottom gradients on fills instead of flat colors. Nodes feel like they float above the background. Connectors get thicker with smooth curves instead of straight lines. Most conservative -- same layout, just deeper.

B

Layered Cards + Glow Accents

Nodes become card-like with a subtle border, inner highlight at the top edge, and a colored glow underneath that matches their phase. Connectors use dashed or dotted animated-looking paths. Background gets a faint dot grid for spatial grounding. More visually rich.

C

Glass Morphism + Phase Regions

Nodes have frosted-glass appearance with semi-transparent backgrounds and blur. Phase groups (Setup, Planning, Execution) get subtle background regions that connect their nodes visually. Connectors are smooth Bezier curves with gradient strokes. Most modern but hardest to get right in pure SVG.
